Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dockit ("the Application") handles user information. The Application is built as an educational guide for yacht docking and vessel manoeuvres.

1. Information Collection and Use

Dockit is designed to run completely offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, device telemetry, location data, or unique identifiers. Any interactive features or calculations execute purely local to your mobile device.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application is distributed through the Google Play Store. While the Application itself contains no internal trackers, analytics frameworks, or advertising SDKs, Google Play Services may collect standard diagnostic device information in accordance with their respective privacy standards.

3. Children's Privacy

Our services do not intentionally target or collect data from individuals under 13 years of age. Because we collect zero data, no child's metrics can be compromised within the framework of this app.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or security concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via email at: support@zottig.de
